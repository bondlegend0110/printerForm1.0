ACTIVITY A - Dream Island with Printerform

Objective:

Encourage students to explore printing different patterns and designs using Printerform with adjustment, as well as experimenting with different forms.

Target Age Group:

Elementary School

Materials/Tools:

Printer, standard 8.5” x 11” paper, glue/stapler, color crayon/pencil/marker, (optional) scissor

Instructions

STEP 1 - INTRODUCTION

  1. Explain what Printerform does and how it works
    • Quick demonstration of the printing Process
    • Talk about consideration in choosing different shapes
    • Explain what each adjustment does
  2. Introduce the activity: create your dream island with Printerform!

STEP 2 - GATHER STL FILES

  1. Supply students with various STL files of houses, animals, etc, OR
  2. Instruct students to create their own STL files or explore existing ones, using platforms such as TinkerCad

STEP 3 - PRINTERFORM WALKTHROUGH

  1. Lead students to go through the entire Printerform process
    • Upload
    • Choose shape
    • experiment with positioning, rotating, angling, and adjusting each STL file
    • Download
  2. After students have downloaded all of their files, print using standard 8.5” x 11” paper

STEP 4 - ASSEMBLE

  1. Ensure students follow the dotted lines and printed direction to fold and create printed prototype
  2. Use glue/stapler to stablize the prototype
  3. Color the prototypes with color crayon/pencil/marker
  4. Let students arrange the prototypes into their dream islands!

STEP 5 - SHARE YOUR WORK

Upload images of finished printed file to our gallery page and help us further develop this tool by providing your feedback at this PrinterForm Survey.
